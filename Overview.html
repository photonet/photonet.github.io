STRATEGY, CREATIVITY AND TECHNOLOGY

Photonet is a team of digital strategists, artists and multimedia technologists working exclusively in digital advertising & content delivery online.
Since its founding in 2016,

 We are photo editing professionals with an in-depth working knowledge of the Adobe Creative cloud (Photoshop).
Our experience specializing in realistic retouching, photo effects and manipulations has taught us that a photo that is worth a thousand words gets you the attentions you  wanted on social and non social platforms.



Our Approach

We believe in a collaborative, multi-phased approach that engages our clients from photo editing project commencement through deployment. Although deliverables are client-specific, each photo editing project follows our proven, well-defined process.



EXECUTE

Build the solution.
Our design and technology teams implement photo editing projects in phases so clients can review and provide feedback throughout the process.

VALIDATE

Make sure it works.
Prior to sharing the final edited photo  with the client, we follow a detailed Test Plan document to ensure the delivered photos meet all project requirements.

PARTNER

Stay connected.
Our team stays engaged with your photo editing projects, identifying enhancement and manipulations while monitoring both performance and friends feedback..
